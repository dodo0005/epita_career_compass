### GPT-OSS 120B (Groq)

After you finish the **MSc Informatique** you are eligible for the **Autorisation Provisoire de Séjour (APS)**. Here's what you need to know:

**What does the APS give you?**
- **Validity:** 12 months (extendable to 24 months in some cases).
- **Work rights:** Up to 964 hours per year (≈20 h/week) in any sector matching your degree level.
- **Transition:** Once you secure a CDI/CDD meeting the Talent‑Passport criteria, you can switch to a Salarié or Talent residence permit.

**How to apply:**
1. Gather documents (passport, current VLS‑TS, proof of graduation, health insurance, proof of address).
2. Submit on the **ANEF portal** (administration-etrangers-en-france.interieur.gouv.fr). A fee applies — check the current amount on ANEF before applying.
3. Apply **before your student permit expires** — at least 2 months in advance.

**Talent Passport threshold:** If you receive a job offer with a salary **≥ 2 × SMIC** (≈€43k gross/year), you can apply directly for the Talent Passport — the fastest work permit route.
